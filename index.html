Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about buying YouTube channels

Is buying a YouTube channel allowed by YouTube?
YouTube allows channel ownership transfers as long as they comply with their terms of service. We ensure all transfers are conducted properly to maintain channel compliance.
How does the Telegram ordering process work?
When you click "Order Now" on a channel, you'll be directed to a Telegram chat with our sales team. From there, we'll discuss details, answer questions, and guide you through the purchase process.
How long does the transfer process take?
Most transfers are completed within 24-72 hours after payment confirmation. We provide step-by-step guidance throughout the entire process.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, cryptocurrency, and escrow services to ensure a secure transaction for both parties.
Can I change the channel name and content after purchase?
Once the channel is yours, you have full creative control. You can rebrand, change content direction, and modify the channel as you see fit.
